Introducing Our new flavor:

Rasberry fudge

Our new flavorful raspberry fudge flavor is hand made in store daily, with fresh picked raspberries imported from our local Houston farmers markets. The raspberries are then blended into a decadent pure, and mixed into our classic vanilla flavor. We then blend in our semi-sweet fudge chocolate chunks to give it a fudge flavor. Enjoy our new new addition to the menu on a cone or as one of our hand crafted milkshakes!
